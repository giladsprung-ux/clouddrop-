Questions? Got answers.

How does the rain cloud actually work?

CloudDrop uses ultrasonic technology to vibrate water at high frequency, creating ultra-fine mist. A pump system simultaneously drops visible water droplets down through the cloud — that's the signature "raining" effect.

How long does the water last?

The 280ml tank runs for approximately 6–8 hours on a single fill. The auto shut-off feature turns the unit off when water runs low.

Is it noisy? Can I sleep with it on?

Yes! The motor operates at under 30dB — quieter than a whisper. Many customers use it specifically as a sleep aid.

Does it work with essential oils?

Absolutely. Add 3–5 drops of any water-soluble essential oil to the tank. Lavender, eucalyptus, and peppermint are customer favorites.

How long does shipping take?

We ship within 24 hours of your order. Standard delivery takes 7–14 days internationally. You'll receive a tracking number via email.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day no-questions-asked return policy. Contact us at support@clouddrop.com and we'll make it right.

Is it safe to leave on overnight?

Yes. The auto shut-off protection activates when the water level drops too low. It uses low-voltage USB power, making it completely safe for overnight use.
